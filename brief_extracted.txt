— Inter or Source Sans Pro. Monospace accents for spec numbers/data.

 Diagonal cut section dividers (not curves — sharp, angular)

Logo: Use the provided LSE logo (red/blue). Place on white/dark nav bar. Ensure minimum clear space.
Global Elements: Sticky header with logo + nav. WhatsApp floating button (bottom-right). Preloader with LSE logo
animation (simple fade/spin). Footer with address, quick links, certifications strip, social links.
